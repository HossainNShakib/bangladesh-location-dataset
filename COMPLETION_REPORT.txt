🆔 ID FORMAT EXAMPLES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Division ID:          1 digit
  Example: 1 (Barishal)

District ID:          3 digits (Division + 2-digit serial)
  Example: 101 (Barishal Division, Serial 01)

SubDistrict ID:       5 digits (District + 2-digit serial)
  Example: 10101 (District 101, Serial 01 = Barishal Sadar)

LocalArea ID:         7 digits (SubDistrict + 2-digit serial)
  Example: 1010101 (SubDistrict 10101, Serial 01 = Kashipur)
